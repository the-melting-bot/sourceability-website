Search Models

A Flexible Approach to Executive Recruiting

01

Executive Search

Our retained executive search is designed for senior-level data and analytics leaders — Chief Data Officers, VPs of Analytics, and Director-level appointments. We conduct thorough market mapping and personalized, confidential outreach to identify and deliver candidates of the highest caliber. You receive our full dedicated attention from kickoff to close.

02

Contingency Search

Performance-based recruiting for mid to senior-level analytics professionals — data scientists, BI developers, data engineers, and analytics managers. You only pay when we deliver the right candidate and they accept your offer. It's a low-risk way to access our specialized network without a retainer commitment.

03

Co-tainer Search

A hybrid model that combines the dedication of retained search with the flexibility of contingency. An initial engagement fee ensures priority placement and deep focus on your role, while the balance is due only upon a successful hire. Ideal for specialized or hard-to-fill data roles where speed and commitment both matter.
