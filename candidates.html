Strategic Career Moves. Long-Term Impact.

We partner with professionals across Data, Technology, and Financial Services to help them make thoughtful, career-defining moves - not simply job changes. Many of the professionals we work with are not actively seeking new opportunities, but are open to the right role when it aligns with their long-term career goals, leadership aspirations, and overall direction.

We believe the right career move should support both immediate opportunity and long-term professional growth.

Who We Work With

We work with experienced professionals and leaders who bring depth of expertise, strong communication skills, and a track record of impact in:

Data & AI

Data Engineering, Analytics, Machine Learning, AI & Automation

Technology

Software Development, Engineering Leadership, Digital Platforms, Infrastructure & Cloud

Financial Services

Commercial & Business Lending, Credit Union Leadership, Risk & Operations

Our focus is on individuals who are thoughtful about their next move and selective about the organizations they choose to join.

What We Look For

We partner with professionals who bring a strong track record of performance and take a thoughtful, long-term approach to their careers.

While every situation is unique, the individuals we work with typically demonstrate:

  • Depth of experience within their field
  • A consistent progression of responsibility and impact
  • Strong communication and leadership capability
  • A focus on long-term fit, not just short-term change

Why Work With SourceAbility

Confidential, Professional Representation

We represent candidates thoughtfully and discreetly in the market. Your background, goals, and timing are handled with professionalism and care.

Access to Opportunities Not Publicly Posted

Many of our searches are conducted on a confidential basis and are never advertised publicly. We provide access to opportunities that are not broadly available through traditional job boards.

Strategic Career Guidance

We help candidates evaluate opportunities based on long-term alignment - role scope, leadership, culture, growth potential, and overall fit, not simply title or compensation.

Market Insight & Honest Feedback

How We Work With You

Our approach is relationship-driven and designed to support professionals over the long term, not simply during an active search.

01

Initial Conversation

We take the time to understand your background, career progression, accomplishments, motivations and what would make your next move meaningful.

02

Ongoing Relationship

We stay connected and share relevant opportunities and market insight as your career evolves.

03

Targeted Introductions

We focus on opportunities that align with your experience, goals, and timing - not mass outreach or mismatched roles.

04

Guidance Through the Process

From initial conversations through interviews, offer evaluation, and final decision-making, we provide perspective and support throughout the process.

We also help candidates think strategically about career direction, market positioning, compensation, and long-term fit, ensuring each opportunity is evaluated thoughtfully, not transactionally.
