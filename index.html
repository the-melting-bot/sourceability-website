Three Proven Search Models

01

Executive Search

Retained search for senior-level data and analytics leaders. We conduct thorough market mapping and targeted outreach to identify and deliver top-tier executive candidates.

02

Contingency Search

Performance-based recruiting for mid to senior-level analytics professionals. You only pay when we deliver the right candidate for your team.

03

Co-tainer Search

A hybrid model combining the dedication of retained search with the flexibility of contingency. Ideal for specialized or hard-to-fill data roles.
